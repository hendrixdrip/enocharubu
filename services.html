My Services

Here is everything I currently take on. If you are not sure which one fits your situation, just send me a message and we can work it out together.

01

Custom Website Design

Every website I build starts from scratch. No page builders, no recycled templates. I design around your brand, your audience, and what you actually need the site to do.

  • Design built around your specific brand
  • Custom page layouts and structure
  • Fast-loading, clean code
  • Works across all major browsers
02

Responsive Design

Most people will visit your site on a phone. Every project I take on is built mobile-first and tested across screen sizes before it goes live.

  • Mobile-first build process
  • Tested on phones, tablets, and desktops
  • Touch-friendly navigation and buttons
  • Images and media that scale properly
03

E-Commerce Solutions

I build online stores for product and service businesses. That includes product pages, payment integration, booking systems, and WhatsApp checkout flows.

  • Product catalog and inventory setup
  • Payment gateway integration
  • Booking and scheduling systems
  • WhatsApp order flow integration
04

Social Media Marketing

I manage Instagram and Facebook for clients who want consistent content without spending their own time on it. That covers the strategy, the visuals, the captions, and the posting schedule.

  • Monthly content plan and calendar
  • Post design and copywriting
  • Instagram and Facebook management
  • Audience growth and engagement
05

SEO & Maintenance

A site that does not show up in search is missing a big chunk of its potential. I handle on-page SEO during the build and offer monthly maintenance for clients who want to keep improving over time.

  • Title tags, meta descriptions, headings, page structure
  • Page speed improvements
  • Security updates and backups
  • Monthly maintenance packages available
06

Website Redesign

If your current site looks outdated or is not converting visitors the way it should, a redesign can make a real difference. I audit what you have, fix what is not working, and rebuild from there.

  • Full audit before any work begins
  • Updated visual design
  • Better navigation and page structure
  • Content carried over from the old site
